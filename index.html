This is how to play the game

  • Choose Operation: Click an operation (+, -, *) to begin.
  • Solve Equation: Enter your answer in the input field.
  • Submit: Click "Submit" to check your answer.
  • Score: Earn points for correct answers.
  • Feedback: "Correct!" or "Wrong!" messages will appear.
  • Repeat: Keep solving until you're done.
  • View Score: Your score is displayed in the score box
  • Help: Refer the instructions for any help
  • End Game: Click "End Game" to exit.
  • Enjoy & Learn: Have fun while improving your math skills!
